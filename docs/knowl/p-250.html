Let \(P_m(z) = \sum_f \pair{P_m(z)}{f}f\) then

\begin{equation*} \pair{P_m(z)}{P_n(z)} = \sum_f \overline {a_f(m)} a_f(n) \frac{\Gamma(k-1)^2}{(4 \pi m)^{k-1} (4\pi n )^{k-1}} \end{equation*}

on the other hand

\begin{equation*} \pair{P_m(z)}{P_n(z)} = \frac{\Gamma(k-1)}{(4\pi m)^{k-1}} \left(\frac mn\right)^{(k-1)/2} \left\{ \cdots \right\}\text{.} \end{equation*}
