  1. \(\{\alpha p_n\}\text{,}\) \(n\)-th primes, \(\alpha\in \RR\smallsetminus \QQ\text{.}\)
  2. \(\{\sqrt{n}\}\)
  3. \(\{\log(n)\}\)
  4. \(\{\log(p_n)\}\)
  5. \(\{\beta^n\}\text{,}\) \(\beta \in \RR\text{.}\)
  6. \(\{\alpha a_n\}\text{,}\) \(a_n \in \ZZ\)
