\(1.\implies 2.\).

\(1.\) means \(2.\) is true for the characteristic function of \(\lb b,c\rb\text{,}\) hence true for step functions (because everything is linear).

Recall that any \(f \in C\lb 0,1\rb)\) is the uniform limit of such functions.
