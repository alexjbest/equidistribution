Theorem 1.8 Duke

Let \(f \in \cinf(\HH)\text{,}\) that is \(\Gamma\) invariant and bounded on \(\mathcal F\text{,}\) then as \(d \to \infty\) over fundamental discriminants

\begin{equation*} \frac{1}{\# \Lambda_d} \sum_{z\in \Lambda_d}{\vphantom{\sum}}^* f(z) \to \iint_{\mathcal F} f\diff \mu\text{.} \end{equation*}
