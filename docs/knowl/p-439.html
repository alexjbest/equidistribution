Let

\begin{equation*} r(a,b,c) = \{ \text{reps. of } ax^2 + bxy + cy^2 \text{ by } x^2 + y^2 + z^2\} \end{equation*}

then

\begin{equation*} r(a,b,c) = 24 \cdot 2^\nu \prod_{p| 2(b^2 - 4ac)} r_p(a,b,c) \end{equation*}

where

\begin{equation*} \nu = \#\text{primes dividing } b^2 - 4ac \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} r_p(a,b,c) = O(1) \text{ unless } p^2 | \gcd(a,b,c) \end{equation*}

for us \(r(d,2e,d)\text{,}\) \(b^2 - 4ac = 4(e^2 - d^2)\) and

\begin{equation*} r(d,2e,d) \ll_\epsilon d^\epsilon\text{.} \end{equation*}
