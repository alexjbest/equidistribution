Waldspurger's formula

Roughly Waldspurger says

\begin{equation*} \frac{|a_{|D|}|}{\pair ff} = \frac{(k - \frac 32)!}{\pi} \frac{|D|^{k-1}}{\pair gg} L(g\otimes \chi_D, k- \frac 12) \end{equation*}

\(f\in S_k(\Gamma_0(4)\) half integral weight, \(f\mapsto g\) Shimura lift, \(a_n\) fourier coeffs of \(F\text{.}\)

Remark 2.59

Shimura lifting takes weight \(k\) to weight \(2k-1\) so \(k- \frac 12\) is the center of the critical strip of

\begin{equation*} L(g \otimes \chi_D, s)\text{.} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} L(g\otimes, \chi_d, s) = \sum \frac{b_n \chi_D(n)}{n^s}\text{.} \end{equation*}
