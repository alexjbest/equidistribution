Theorem 1.28 Weyl

\(\{a_n\}_{n=1}^\infty \in \lb 0 ,1 \rb\) then TFAE:

  1. \(\{a_n\}_{n=1}^\infty\) is uniformly distributed.
  2. \(\forall f \in C(\lb 0,1\rb)\)
    \begin{equation*} \lim_{N\to \infty} \frac 1N \sum_{n=1}^N f(a_n) = \int _0^1 f \diff x\text{.} \end{equation*}
  3. \begin{equation*} \lim_{N\to \infty} \frac 1N \sum_{n=1}^N e(ma_n) = \delta_m \end{equation*}

where \(e(x) = e^{2\pi i x}\text{,}\)

\begin{equation*} \delta_m = \begin{cases} 0 \amp m \ne 0 \\ 1 \amp m = 0 \end{cases}\text{.} \end{equation*}
